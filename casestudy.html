improvement using data-driven insights.

Client Overview

PNWInvest.com was marketing for motivated seller leads in the Portland metro area. This type of lead is competitive with the average cost per click being as high as $50 for some keywords.

In order to compete, it’s important to have ads that catch the user’s eye and website that converts paid traffic into leads.

To start - the client setup a simple Google ads campaign so they could see some baseline data. This ad ran for an entire quarter before being paused so they could evaluate their budget, strategy and projections.

The data that came in showed a handful of keywords that were producing a majority of the clicks. Our insights suggested they increase their spend on those keywords in order to maximize their budget and increase the amount of clicks that would potentially turn into leads.

Keyword Performance

After 4 consecutive months of running this optimized campaign it was clear that the “sell my house fast for cash” keyword was producing the majority of the results for this client.

They moved their bidding strategy to a point where they were showing ads for nearly every search for this keyword in their target market.

The cost per click was as high as $39 but the underlying data suggested this was a worthy investment.

Mobile User Insights

One recommendation that we made from our data suggested that a majority of the impressions, clicks and conversions were coming from mobile devices as seen in the data below.

With this information we suggested some changes to their website to make it easier to submit a lead generation form on a mobile device.

We also recommended text message follow up immediately because you are equipped with the knowledge the client is on their phone.

Campaign ROI

The client reported that they were able to close 3 deals from these 17 leads. The profits from these deals exceeded $15,000 with a $5,570 marketing budget. Excluding other expenses like their time, software, and other factors we didn’t have insight - we helped them calculate a 169% ROI from this one marketing campaign.
